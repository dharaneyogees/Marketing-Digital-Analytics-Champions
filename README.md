#  Marketing & Digital Analytics Champions

##  Executive Business Problem
The marketing team flagged high top-of-funnel engagement across social channels, but overall conversion rates in our premium **Health & Wellness segment** were stagnating below the corporate benchmark of **8.00%**. This project acts as a strategic diagnostic to spot exactly where the revenue was leaking.

## The Analytics Story 

### Phase 1: The Trap (The Macro View)
Users enter our marketing pipeline through various channels like Email, Search, and Social Media. 
* On the surface, top-of-funnel traffic metrics looked incredible. 
* However, granular data aggregations revealed that while steady channels like Email and Search safely stabilized at a **8.06% conversion rate**, our highest-budget channel—**Influencer Marketing**—dropped significantly to **7.79%**. 
* Premium influencer spends were actively diluting our baseline profitability.

### Phase 2: The Geographic Black Holes (Where is the Leak?)
We deep-diverged into regional performance metrics specifically for the underperforming influencer campaigns. 
* The numbers exposed two massive regional bottlenecks: **Miami (7.51%)** and **Los Angeles (7.51%)** were the absolute lowest-performing territories.
* **The Business Root Cause:** Influencers in these specific coastal hubs focus heavily on lifestyle aesthetics rather than product transparency. Customers click the link out of curiosity, but bounce immediately when faced with a lack of detailed product info.

### Phase 3: The Timeline Fatigue (The Broken Trust Blueprint)
Finally, we analyzed campaign lifecycles (15, 30, 45, and 60 days) to see how duration affects consumer behavior.
* The data showed sharp drops at the **15-day** and **45-day** marks.
* **The Consumer Behavior Insight:** For health and wellness products, consumers need continuous social proof. Short 15-day bursts expire before building brand conviction, while awkward 45-day extensions suffer from audience fatigue without proper narrative resets.

## Data-Driven Resolution & Action Plan
To maximize our ROI and fix the broken funnel paths across our acquisition touchpoints, we executed three strategic pivots:

1. **Immediate Capital Freeze:** Suspended underperforming short-term influencer contracts in Miami and Los Angeles.
2. **Budget Re-routing:** Reallocated that leaked capital into high-performing, long-term (30-60 Days) automated Email sequencing and targeted Search ads.
3. **Content Brief Reset:** Restructured influencer guidelines to mandate educational transparency over generic lifestyle placement.
